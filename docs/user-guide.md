# User Guide

## Purpose

Pharmacy App helps staff manage inventory, sales, suppliers, and customers, with role-based access.

## Roles and Access

- Admin: Full access to all modules and configuration.
- Manager: Operational access similar to admin without system-level changes.
- Cashier / Pharmacist / Other: Point-of-sale (POS) access only.

Note: Non-admin/manager users are routed directly to POS and do not see the sidebar or protected modules.

## Core Workflows

### 1) Login

1. Open the login screen.
2. Enter username and password.
3. You will be routed by role:
   - Admin/Manager: Dashboard
   - Other roles: POS

### 2) POS Sales

1. Open the POS screen.
2. Search products by barcode.
3. Add items, quantities, and complete the sale.
4. Confirm payment method and status.

### 3) Products and Stock

1. Go to Products.
2. Add new products with required details.
3. Add stock batches to maintain inventory levels.

### 4) Suppliers

1. Go to Suppliers.
2. Create a supplier.
3. Use supplier search to view details and update records.

### 5) Purchase Orders

1. Open Purchasing Order screen.
2. Create a PO by selecting supplier and expected date.
3. View current purchase orders in the table.

### 6) Customers

1. Go to Customers.
2. Add customer details.
3. Review customer activity and loyalty info.

## Password Reset

If you forgot your password:

1. Click “Forgot Password?” on the login page.
2. Enter the email used at account creation.
3. You will receive a reset link (if the email exists).
4. Use the link to set a new password.

## Common Issues

- Invalid credentials: Check username and password.
- Access denied: Ensure your role is allowed for the page.
- Missing data: Verify required fields are filled.

## Support

Contact your system administrator for account or access changes.
